Innovative Technology for a Dynamic World

Vision

To be a leading University in quality training, strategic research, innovation and technology development and transfer

Mission

To train and develop human resources equipped with innovative skills to inspire enterprise in the disciplines of engineering, science, health and technology, to meet the demands of a dynamic world.

Core Values

Professionalism, Integrity, transparency and accountability, Innovation and creativity, Excellence & Equity

Motto

Innovative technology for a dynamic world.

Why Study at KyU?

KyU is positioning itself to be a leader in offering courses geared towards producing employers and not employees in areas outlined by the Vision 2030.

Kirinyaga University set in a welcoming and accessible forested environment, 1hr 30 min drive from Nairobi, is far enough to offer serene studying environment but close enough for convenience.

We offer market sensitive Programmes tailored to careers in IT, Health Science, Engineering, Business, Pure and Applied Sciences, Hospitality, Architecture and the Built Environment.
